How to ensure a clear audio recording for transcription

I type between 100,000 to 150,000 words per week. Most of this involves the transcription of audio recordings to an electronic document. I transcribe interviews, focus groups for research, university lectures, therapist consultations and every now and then, a YouTube video, plus many other types of voice recordings. One of my objectives when I transcribe is to have as few [inaudible] *00:00:34 or [over talk] 00:00:35 time stamps, or [I cannot quite understand what the speaker is saying] *00:00:37 throughout the document. Sometimes these are simply unavoidable, but can be quite easily prevented when recording your audio.
