Virtual Assistance Coordinnaire

Do you have better things to do and find yourself needing a spare someone to help with those tasks which keep getting in your way? Imagine a Personal Assistant or Executive Assistant right there at your fingertips to help you with:

  • Research
  • Document Preparation
  • Report Writing
  • Typing, editing, proofreading
  • Becoming or staying organised
  • Decluttering your workplace
  • Project management
  • Accounts receivable and chasing debts

Just like an office assistant without the hassle of extra office space, office equipment, income tax, superannuation, workers compensation, annual and personal leave.
